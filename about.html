About Homee IT

Homee IT is a scheme and program that is non-profit oriented, aimed at delivering your dream home, no matter your budget - because we believe everyone has the right to quality standard of living.


Homee IT was founded by Ms. Praise Fatoye and developed by a group of Real Estate professionals.

Homee IT has helped countless families own their houses, and many others have secured standard rented apartments at reasonable and affordable prices. Due to our various payment plans, both the rich and the richer can all have the home of their dreams.


Our clients satisfaction is our utmost goal.

Vision

To be the reference point for first grade real estates and real estate investments in Abuja Nigeria, setting standards of excellence in the industry.




Mission

Acheiving success in creating ideal homes for both the rich and the richer. Building high quality infrastructures in the state, thereby encouraging foreign investors who will enjoy staying in our homes.
